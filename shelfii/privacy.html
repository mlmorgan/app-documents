Privacy Policy

Shelfii — Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains how Shelfii collects, uses, and protects your information. We have written it to be clear and readable.

Who we are

Shelfii is developed and operated by Matthew Morgan ("we", "us", or "our"). If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at mlmorgan4859@gmail.com.

What information we collect

Account information
When you create an account, we collect your email address. We also store the profile information you choose to provide, such as your username, display name, bio, and profile photo.

Username
When you complete onboarding you choose a username. Your username is permanent, forms your public profile URL (for example, shelfii.app/yourusername), and is visible to other users. Choose it carefully as it cannot be changed.

Sign in with Google
If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address from Google to create or authenticate your account. We do not receive your Google password.

Your collections and content
When you use Shelfii, we store the collections and items you create, including any titles, descriptions, notes, and photos you upload. Collections are private by default and only shared if you explicitly make them public. Photos you upload are compressed on your device before being sent to our servers.

Emoji reactions
When you react to a public collection with an emoji, we store the emoji you used and associate it with your account. Other users can see which emoji reactions a collection has received and which accounts placed each reaction.

Photos and camera
If you choose to upload photos from your device or take a photo within the app, we request access to your camera or photo library. This access is only used to attach photos to your items. We do not access your camera or photos at any other time. Photos are compressed locally on your device before upload and are not sent to any third party for processing.

Crash reports and diagnostics
If the app crashes, anonymised diagnostic data may be collected by Apple or Google through their standard platform frameworks. We do not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting services.

We do not collect

  • Your contacts or communications
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Your location
  • Any data for advertising or profiling purposes

How we use your information

We use the information collected solely to provide and improve Shelfii:

  • Your email is used to manage your account and send essential messages such as password resets
  • Your username and profile information are displayed to other users as part of your public profile
  • Your collections and items are stored and displayed as part of the service
  • Emoji reactions are displayed on public collections to show engagement
  • Crash data is used to fix technical issues and improve stability

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it for any purpose beyond providing the service.

Third-party services

Shelfii uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — provides our database, authentication, and file storage. Your account data, collections, items, and uploaded photos are stored on Supabase infrastructure.
  • Google — if you choose to sign in with Google, your email address is shared with us by Google to authenticate your account. The app also uses Google Fonts to load typefaces, which involves a request to Google's font servers on first use.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — distribute the app. See Apple and Google's respective privacy policies.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Collection and profile visibility

Collections are private by default and only visible to you. You can choose to make a collection public, at which point it becomes visible to anyone and may appear in the Explore section. Photos in public collections are stored with publicly accessible URLs. If you make a collection public, do not include personal information in names, item descriptions, or notes that you would not want to be publicly visible.

Your username and any display name, bio, or profile photo you set are visible to other users of the app. Your public collections are listed on your public profile page.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all of your data including collections, items, reactions, and uploaded photos is permanently deleted.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can delete your account and all associated data from the Settings page within the app. For any other data requests or questions, contact us at mlmorgan4859@gmail.com.

Children's privacy

Shelfii is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Shelfii after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this privacy policy, please contact us at mlmorgan4859@gmail.com.
