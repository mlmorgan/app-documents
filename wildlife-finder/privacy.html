Privacy Policy

Wildlife Finder — Last updated: June 2025

This policy explains how Wildlife Finder collects, uses, and protects your information. We have written it to be clear and readable rather than full of legal jargon.

Who we are

Wildlife Finder is developed and operated by Matthew Morgan ("we", "us", or "our"), based in the United Kingdom. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at mlmorgan4859@gmail.com.

What information we collect

Location data
When you grant permission, the app accesses your device's location to show wildlife recorded near you and to scope charts and data to your local area. Your precise location is used in real time to make requests to the iNaturalist API and is not stored on our servers. Location data is never used for advertising or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Species search queries
When you search for or tap on a species, the species' common name and scientific name are sent to Anthropic's API to generate expert field notes. No personal information is included in these requests. Anthropic processes this data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Subscription information
If you subscribe to Wildlife Finder, your payment is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment card details. Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat, which receives a non-identifying subscription identifier from Apple or Google to confirm your access level.

Crash reports and diagnostics
If the app crashes, anonymised diagnostic data may be collected by Apple or Google through their standard platform frameworks. This helps us identify and fix technical problems.

We do not collect

  • Your name, email address, or any account information (no registration is required)
  • Photos or media from your device
  • Contacts or communications
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Any data for advertising or profiling purposes

How we use your information

We use the information collected solely to provide and improve the Wildlife Finder service:

  • Location is used to load nearby species data from iNaturalist and to scope charts to your area
  • Species names are sent to Anthropic to generate field notes and expert tips
  • Subscription status is used to determine which features are available to you
  • Crash data is used to fix technical issues and improve stability

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioural profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app's core functionality.

Third-party services

Wildlife Finder uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • iNaturalist — provides wildlife observation data. Your location coordinates are included in requests to their API to retrieve nearby species. See iNaturalist's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — generates AI-powered field notes using species names. No personal data is sent. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription status. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — handle payment processing and distribute the app. See Apple and Google's respective privacy policies.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — used for location name lookup if you search for a place. See OpenStreetMap Foundation's Privacy Policy.

Data retention

Because Wildlife Finder does not operate accounts or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data retained by us to delete. Your location is processed transiently during app use and is not stored. Your app preferences (such as your chosen radius and time window) are stored locally on your device only and can be cleared by deleting the app.

Your rights under UK GDPR

As a UK resident, you have the following rights regarding personal data we process:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to object — you can object to certain types of processing
  • Right to data portability — you can ask for your data in a portable format

Given that we hold minimal personal data and none on our own servers, most of these rights are exercised through your device settings or the third-party services above. To make a request or ask a question, contact us at mlmorgan4859@gmail.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe your data has been handled improperly.

Children's privacy

Wildlife Finder is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

Location permissions

The app requests "When In Use" location permission only — your location is never accessed in the background. You can revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. If you deny location permission, the app will still function but will require you to search for a location manually.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users within the app. Continued use of Wildlife Finder after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this privacy policy, please contact us at mlmorgan4859@gmail.com.
